How it works

Up and running before the lunch rush.

No technical knowledge needed. If you can take a photo on your phone, you've already got this.

STEP 01
📷

Photograph your menu

Take a clear photo of your existing printed menu — laminated, blackboard, or paper all work.

STEP 02
✨

AI reads it for you

Claude AI scans the photo and extracts every item, price, and section heading in seconds. Fix anything it missed.

STEP 03
🖨️

Print the QR code

Download the QR and print it. Tape it to a table tent or laminate it — no special hardware needed.

STEP 04
📊

Watch orders roll in live

Your kitchen dashboard updates the moment customers order. Move tickets through New → Preparing → Ready with a single tap.

📱

Scan the QR on your table

Open your camera and point it at the code. The menu loads in your browser — no app to download.

🛒

Build your order

Tap items to add them, adjust quantities, and add any notes for the kitchen.

🚀

Send to the kitchen

Tap send and your order goes straight to the restaurant. Then watch it move from Received → Preparing → Ready, live on your screen.
